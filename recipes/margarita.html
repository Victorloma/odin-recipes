Margarita

How to make a classic Margarita!

This iconic drink will brings every party to the next level! It is super simple and unbelievably delicious. If you wanna impress your friends or family at the next get together, this is drink is the way to go!

What you need:

    • 3cl of tequila
      3cl of triple sec (Cointreau)
      3cl of lime juice
      Ice cubes
      A bit of salt for the rim
      1 lime wedge

    • How to make this delicous beverage:

    • Step 1: Use the lime wedge to wet the rim of the Margarita glass, then dip of roll it in a dish of salt until the edge of the glass is completely covered with salt.
      Step 2: Add the tequila, the Cointreau and the lime juice into a shaker.
      Step 3: Fill your shaker with ice cubes.
      Step 4: Then shake the drink until your hands are freezing. The drink needs to be ice cold!
      step 5: Now you strain and poor the drink into the Margarita glass.
      Step 6: If you want, you can add a lime wedge as garnish!
      Step 7: Enjoy your Margarita!
